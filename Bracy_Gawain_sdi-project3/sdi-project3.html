Project 3

The Theme that I chose is, "What type of tracks will I make for Ciara". I chose this because it relates to what I do as a profession and I feel is a good match in seeing how it can relate to Javascript.

My current career consists of composing music for major recording artists. My current project is with Ciara. While working on this project I came to realization of how variables can come into play while creating music for her. I am making a decision on whether I will do Urban R&B tracks or Pop R&B tracks.

For starters, when I begin making a track I either begin with a melody or a drum pattern. If I start with a melody then usually the instrumental will result in a melodic progressive driven composition and the drums will complement that melody which is found more often in todays top 40 pop music. If I chose to start a drum pattern first in most cases the instrumental will be drum driven and the melody will be simple and will complement those drums which is found in today's urban R&B music. Typically it takes a shorter amount of time to compose an Urban R&B track averaging 2 hours.

Once I decide which route I will take, I decide what type of drums will be used for the instrumental. Depending my drum choice will place the instrumental in a musical genre. If I choose more bass heavy (808) drums sounds the instrumental can be categorized as urban. If I choose more organic/reverb drums the instrumental will be categorized as pop. Typically it takes a longer amount of time to compose a Pop R&B track averaging 4 hours.

I would like at least 2 records on the album. In accomplishing this goal I will create 4 more instrumentals than needed for the project for variety purposes, with a total of 6.

While making the intial tracks I began creating. Ciara made a decision to do a combination of 1 Urban R&b and 1 Pop R&b track for the two tracks needed for her album.
